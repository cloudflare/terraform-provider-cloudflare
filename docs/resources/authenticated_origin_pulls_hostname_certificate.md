# cloudflare_authenticated_origin_pulls_hostname_certificate (Resource)

Manages a hostname-level client certificate for Authenticated Origin Pulls. These certificates can be associated with specific hostnames to enable per-hostname authenticated origin pulls. A maximum of 10 hostname certificates per zone are allowed.
